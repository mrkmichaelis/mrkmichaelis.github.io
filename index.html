01 — Featured
Unity C# Final Project
Starsea Routes
Gameplay Designer · Systems Designer · Unity C# Developer

A 2D space survival prototype about navigating a procedurally-placed moving portal while protecting cargo and managing limited directional information. Every run is unique — the route is never stable, so the player constantly adjusts strategy.

Core Systems
Player System

8-direction WASD movement with rotation to match direction. Clean intro animation on level start.

Cargo Health

Health visualized as cargo states (Full → Damaged → Critical). Thematic and intuitive for the space survival genre.

Enemy AI

Two pirate types: simple chasers and greater pirates that predict player movement and fire cannonballs ahead of position.

Orienting System

A timed arrow reveals portal direction, then recharges. Creates cycles of clear information and uncertainty that scale with difficulty.

Hazard System

Asteroids and black holes that spawn based on player position and increase pressure at higher levels.

Procedural Design

No fixed maps. Portal distance scales per level and can teleport mid-run, forcing constant route recalculation.

Support Pickups
  • Resupply — restores cargo
  • Clues — speeds up arrow recharge
  • Telescope — expands camera view
Design Focus
  • Systems-driven replayability
  • Readable visual feedback
  • Difficulty that scales cleanly
  • Movement that feels direct
